About Us

Maurya Palace is situated at unique location which is near from the Mau Junction railway station and roadways busstop and at the same time it is also away from the city hustle and bustle. We are positioned in the middle of the beauty of nature, surrounded by lush greenery all around and fresh air in barpur of Mau city. We provide bookings for marriage, birthday, reception ceremony, and corporate function. We offer two types of venues namely wedding hall and lawn. The wedding hall provides ample space for jaimala ceremony, tilak ceremony, and cake cutting ceremony. In addition, to that, there is adequate room for sitting the guests in hall. The lawn provides enough place for dinning, revolving hydraulic vermala stage, DJ, and parking.

Hall

Our hall area can be used for jaimala ceremony, tilak ceremony, reception ceremony, and cake cutting ceremony.

Lawn

Our lawn area can be used for dinning, revolving hydraulic vermala stage, DJ night, and parking for guests vechiles.
